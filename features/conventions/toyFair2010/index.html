Welcome to Toy Fair, 2010!
 
 
The Preserve is very proud to present coverage of the Toy Fair, 2010!
 
 
 
Day 3: Feb. 16th, 2010
  • BC USA - Featuring their iwako eraser line, and a number of cool models.
  • Diamond Select - So many products, and now the official USA Playmates distributor!
  • Fan Stamp - Like a temporary tattoo, but better!
  • Kotobukiya - New Star Wars chopsticks and more great figures and statues!
  • Nowstalgic Toys - Squirmles, and the young owner's DeLorean!
  • Puppetoys - Great movie-quality puppets of dinos and sharks!
  • Shidonni - Custom hand made plush toys, designed by the kids themselves!
  • Shocker Toys - Mallows, Indy Spotlight and more!
  • SplashJack - Waterproof floating playing cards - make a Liger and I'm sold!
  • SquareEnix - FFXIII comes out soon - take a look at the products from the franchise!
  • Street Surfing - Skateboard meets surfboard meets street!
  • Underground Toys - New Doctor Who figures, and some Torchwood too.
 
Day 2: Feb. 15th, 2010
  • Funko - A new line of plush figures joins their bobbling ranks!
  • Joojoo - A new plush Noah and his ark!
  • Jun Planning - New Pullip dolls galore!
  • Mezco Toyz - General - Some Little Big Planet and Family Guy.
  • Mezco Toyz - Cinema of Fear - Jason and Myers and Wolfman oh my!
  • Mezco Toyz - Green Hornet - A few prototype pics.
  • Mezco Toyz - Kick Ass - One of the hottest properties of 2010.
  • Mezco Toyz - Living Dead Dolls - This line keeps selling out immediately.
  • Mezco Toyz - Mezitz - Their top seller - the Mezitz rock!
  • NECA - Bioshock - Figures from the popular game.
  • NECA - Gears of War - The popular game-inspired line continues.
  • NECA - General - Amazing Predator and T-1000 sculpts!
  • NECA - Gremlins - When exactly does it become not after midnight?
  • NECA - Street Fighter - Line up your quarters for this great ongoing line.
  • Playmobil - Their new castle and dragon theme is hot - go see!
  • Schleich - New additions to their Smurf line.
  • Tonner - (part 1) Doctor Who and Torchwood revealed at last!
  • Tonner - (part 2) More Tonner goodness.
  • Treasure Chest Pets - Stash your goodies in these plush animals.
 
Day 1: Feb. 14th, 2010
  • (General) - A few general pics from the show.
  • Basic Fun - No more Madballs, but now with Koosh!
  • Lego - General - Pictures from Lego's showroom.
  • Lego - Atlantis - Building sets under da sea.
  • Lego - Ben10 - New figures from the popular brand.
  • Lego - Bionicle - The last gasp for this amazingly creative line.
  • Lego - Bricks & More - Building sets, more akin to the good old days.
  • Lego - Cars - The branded set expansion continues.
  • Lego - City - Great new sets, and the advent calendar!
  • Lego - Creator - What can you create?
  • Lego - Duplo - Lego, safe for little kids.
  • Lego - Harry Potter - Wizard themed sets.
  • Lego - Hero Factory - Did someone say Bionicle?.
  • Lego - Kingdoms - Some fantastic castles.
  • Lego - Lego Games - A new initiative - boardgames from Lego.
  • Lego - LegoVille - Village sets from Duplo.
  • Lego - Mini Sets - Small sets perfect for the checkout lane.
  • Lego - Power Miners - Year two of the popular underground line.
  • Lego - Prince of Persia - The decade-spanning game franchise, now in brick!
  • Lego - Racers - This line continues to deliver the good looking cars.
  • Lego - Space Police - Police! In space! Purple alien Pimp!
  • Lego - Star Wars - The marquee brand carries forth with great new sets.
  • Lego - Technic - Gears and cogs, and several new sets.
  • Lego - Toy Story - With the third movie coming soon, everyone is on board!
  • Lego - World Racers - Another great racing themed line.
  • Mattel - General - Coverage of Mattel's Collector event!
  • Mattel - Avatar - The highest grossing movie of all time is going to move some product.
  • Mattel - Barbie - Celebrating the 125 careers of Barbie through the ages.
  • Mattel - Batman: The Brave and the Bold - One of our favorite new lines continues.
  • Mattel - Big Foot - Dancing Big Foot.
  • Mattel - Cars - Disney continues to sell well.
  • Mattel - DC Retro Action - A cool new line of Mego-ish figure for TRU exclusives.
  • Mattel - DC Super Friends - Aimed at younger kids, with Batman themes.
  • Mattel - DC Universe Classics - The hard to find series grows!
  • Mattel - DC Universe - Smaller Scale than the Classics, but packed with articulation.
  • Mattel - Ghostbusters - PhDs in Parapsychology and psychology.
  • Mattel - Hot Wheels - Check out the vintage re-release of the clover-leaf track!
  • Mattel - Justice League Unlimited - The unlimitedly large set gets larger - with Lobo!
  • Mattel - Masters of the Universe Classics - Surprises galore!
  • Mattel - The Sing-a-ma-jigs! - There are gonna be huge - you watch.
  • Mattel - Toy Story 3 - With the movie on the way, here are the toys.
  • Mattel - Wheelies - Best show demo ever.
  • Mattel - WWE - Roddy Piper and the Sarge!
 
Day 0: Feb. 13th, 2010
  • Hasbro - General - Pictures from Hasbro's showroom.
  • Hasbro - Chuck & Friends - construction inspired figures.
  • Hasbro - G.I. Joe - Start Pursuing Cobra
  • Hasbro - Ironman 2 - Figures in support of the upcoming movie.
  • Hasbro - Littlest PetShop - Now with Blythe cross-overs.
  • Hasbro - Marvel - Legends, comic packs and 19" Galactus!
  • Hasbro - Mr. Potato Head & Wheel Pals - Tony Starch = best product name of 2010.
  • Hasbro - Nerf - Get ready for 9-9-10!
  • Hasbro - Spiderman - New Spidey figures keep the line going.
  • Hasbro - Strawberry Shortcake - The sweet smelling little figures with great playsets.
  • Hasbro - Star Wars - The line goes strong into 2010, with giant AT-AT!
  • Hasbro - Super Hero Squad - Mini figures in this line are still doing well.
  • Hasbro - Tonka - Classic styling along with newly designed vehicles.
  • Hasbro - Transformers - New combiners and powerish-masters.
  • Hasbro - Transformers Speed Stars - Another extension of the Transformers brand.
  • Hasbro - Weebles - They wobble, but don't fall down!.
